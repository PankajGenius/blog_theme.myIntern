E-O-D Adventure Park: A Thrilling Adventure in the Heart of Delhi

Hello, fellow adventure enthusiasts!

I'm Pankaj, and I recently embarked on an exhilarating journey to the E-O-D Adventure Park, nestled near the serene Sanjay Lake in Mayur Vihar, New Delhi. I couldn't wait to share this unforgettable experience with you.

E-O-D Adventure Park is conveniently located at Pocket D, Mayur Vihar, New Delhi, with the nearest metro station being Mayur Vihar Phase 1. Its proximity to public transport makes it easily accessible to both locals and tourists alike. As I entered the park, I could feel the excitement building up.

Thrills Await!

The moment I stepped into E-O-D Adventure Park, I was greeted by a world of adventure and fun. This park is a paradise for both kids and adults, offering a wide range of thrilling games and activities that cater to all ages. The sheer variety of options left me spoiled for choice.

Lakeshore Activities

The Lakeshore Activity Center at the park is a delightful surprise. It offers boating on the tranquil Sanjay Lake, a game of bowling with friends, and an archery range where you can channel your inner Robin Hood. The serene lakeside setting adds a touch of tranquility to the overall excitement.

Rope Courses & More

For the daredevils among us, E-O-D Adventure Park offers a series of rope courses that will test your courage and agility. Climb, swing, and balance your way through the treetop courses while soaking in the picturesque views of the lake and surrounding greenery.
